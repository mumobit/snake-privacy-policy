Snake Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 October 2025

Thank you for using Snake ("the App"), developed by Mumobit. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that may be collected and how it is used when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you when you use the App. The App is designed to operate without requiring you to share any personal information. We do use Google Analytics and Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect and store anonymous usage data and crash reports to help improve our user experience. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

2. Ads in the App

Our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads may collect non-personally identifiable information to deliver relevant advertisements to you. Please note that AdMob may use cookies and other technologies to track user interactions with ads. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. Payment Information

If you choose to purchase the one-time payment to remove ads in the App, all payment transactions are processed by the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Third-Party Links

Our App may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. Please review the privacy policies of those websites or services to understand how they collect and handle your data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy within the App or on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mumobit@gmail.com.
